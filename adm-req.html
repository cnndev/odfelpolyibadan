Admission Requirements

THE POLYTECHNIC IBADAN OPEN DISTANCE AND FLEXIBLE e-LEARNING.



  • 1. HND Programme:

Candidate must have National Diploma (ND) with a minimum of lower credit pass in the discipline obtained from NBTE accredited program plus a minimum of one year post ND work experience. Candidate with ND at pass level and a minimum of two year post ND work experience.


  • 2. ND Programme:

Candidate must have scored the minimum cut off mark specified by the Joint Admission and Matriculation Board.
In addition to the above, all candidates must have obtained SSCE/GCE O'Level (NECO/NABTEB/Equivalent) in five subjects relevant (as indicated below) to the program at not more than two sittings.
