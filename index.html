Benefits of ODFel
Programme!

The Polytechnic, Ibadan Open, Distance, and Flexible e-Learning (ODFeL) gives students the freedom to learn from anywhere, anytime & anyhow. It enables you to deal with office, businesses, family, and other constraints that may affect your conventional study at the campus.

Study from Anywhere at any time

Our programs are available online, so you may study whenever and from anywhere you choose.

Easy Course Access

Students will have 24/7 access to lectures and course contents because our programmes are run online.

Flexible Tuition Fee

Affordable tuition and flexible payment methods in comparison to on-campus programmes, Poly Ibadan ODFeL courses are often less expensive.

Uninterrupted academic calendar

At The Polytechnic Ibadan ODFeL academic calendar is strictly followed with immediate online results view by students.

Same Certificates

Our ODFeL programmes have the same level of quality, support, and certification. The ODFeL programme at The Polytechnic Ibadan is open to both Nigerian and International students.

NYSC Mobilization of Graduands

Our Graduands at Poly Ibadan ODFeL will be mobilize for NYSC.





HOW TO APPLY


Step 1 >

Click Apply Now

Step 2 >

Pay Application Fees for The Polytechnic Ibadan ODFeL ND, HND or Certification Programmes.

  • For National Diploma, the application form costs ₦2,000 .
  • For Higher National Diploma, the application form costs ₦5,000.
  • For Certification Programmes, the application form costs ₦2,000 .
  • All fees must be paid through The Polytechnic Ibadan ODFeL portal.

    • Step 3 >

    Proceed to the application portal to upload your school documents and biodata, you will be required to provide scanned copies of your credentials (education information and referee form).

    Step 4 >

    Fill the application form online with valid email address and telephone number, then click the Submit for Review Button.

    Step 5 >

    After submission, a reference number will be provided for you and your provided information will be displayed for confirmation. Accept terms and conditions and submit your application form by pressing Submit Button.

    Step 6 >

    Print your Application Form.

    Step 7 >

    Your Poly Ibadan ODFeL application form will be processed by our admission team. After you have satisfied our requirements, your admission into Poly Ibadan ODFeL will be granted by our ODFeL Centre.

    NOTE

    • • Applicants for National Diploma Programmes must submit their JAMB details and other necessary documents through our dedicated portal above, ODFeL screening fee is #2,000 only.
    • • Admitted students will be required to pay Acceptance Fees and Tuition Fees, check www.odfel.polyibadan.edu.ng for tuition fees details.
    • • Higher National Diploma applicants must possess National Diploma from accredited Institutions in relevant programmes to qualify for admission into The Polytechnic Ibadan ODFeL HND programme.
    • • Digital passport photograph and relevant credentials are required to be uploaded at the application portal.
    • • All successful candidates will be provided with a softcopy of admission letter, your students account login details will be generated and will be sent to your valid email address. PLEASE ensure you change your default password to the one you will always remember. DO NOT Disclose your password to anybody.
